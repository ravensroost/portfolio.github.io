What is Genre in Boardgames?

What information can be extracted from the wealth of data on the popular boardgame database, Board Game Geek? This analysis aims to learn more about what kinds of games players most enjoy, and what quantifiable measures distinguish games in one genre from another. How have games changed over time? Can factors such as player count, complexity, popularity, and play time be used to predict a game’s rating by players?
